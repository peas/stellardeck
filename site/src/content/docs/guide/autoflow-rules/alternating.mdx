## When it fires

- The slide has **3 or more paragraphs** (4 paragraphs would hit z-pattern first)
- Each paragraph is short (≤10 words, ≤2 lines)

## What it does

Adds `[.alternating-colors: true]`. Each paragraph alternates between the
heading color and the accent color, giving rhythm to a "three points" or
"five forces" slide without any explicit positioning.

## Why 3 (not 4)

If a slide has exactly 4 short paragraphs, the [z-pattern](../z-pattern/)
rule wins first (priority 40 vs 50). Use 3 or 5+ paragraphs to get
alternating colors instead.
