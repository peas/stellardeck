## When it fires

- The slide is **slide 0** of the deck (guard: `info.index === 0`)
- The first paragraph is a single line, ≤6 words, plain text
- There's at least one more paragraph below it
- The total subtitle words are **greater than** the title words
  (otherwise it's a statement, not title+subtitle)

## What it does

- Wraps the title line in `#[fit]`
- Adds `[.heading-align: center]`
- Result: a centered, fit-sized title with a subtitle below
