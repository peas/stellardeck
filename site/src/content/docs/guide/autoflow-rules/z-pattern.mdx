## When it fires

- The slide has **exactly 4 paragraphs**
- Each paragraph is short (≤8 words, ≤2 lines)

## What it does

The 4 paragraphs are placed at the 4 corners of the slide:

```
top-left          top-right

bottom-left   bottom-right
```

Reads as a Z scan pattern. Used for "four pillars" or "four steps" content.
